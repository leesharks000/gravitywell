## Five Billable Primitives

The pricing meters five things. Everything else is free.

1. **Active chains** — how many provenance streams you maintain
2. **Captures** — staged objects (the raw material)
3. **Deposits** — Zenodo-anchored, wrapped or raw
4. **Invoke tokens** — LLM calls through GW (free with BYOK)
5. **Encrypted operations** — private/hash-only captures

This matches the product's actual value creation. You pay for continuity, compression, and privacy — not for reading your own data.
